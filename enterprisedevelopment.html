Enterprise Development

The art and handicraft sector is the second largest employment generator after agriculture in India but the actual producer or manufacturer suffers due to involvement of middle men, lack of knowledge in marketing, lack of stock management and no involvement of technology. Thus we support young individuals and group of entrepreneurs in incubation of idea, building product, increasing visibility, market linkage and increasing the sale.
